Betting Glossary

Expected Value (EV) ‚ñº
The theoretical average outcome of a bet if it were made repeatedly. Positive EV bets are profitable long-term, while negative EV bets lose money over time. Our AI calculates EV for every recommendation.
Sharp Money ‚ñº
Wagers placed by professional or highly skilled bettors who consistently beat the market. Sharp money often causes line movements and indicates where the smart money is going.
Kelly Criterion ‚ñº
Mathematical formula for determining optimal bet sizing based on your edge and bankroll. Helps maximize long-term growth while minimizing risk of ruin.
Closing Line Value (CLV) ‚ñº
Measure of how good your betting line was compared to the final closing line. Consistently beating closing lines indicates sharp betting ability.
