Parlay Betting

What is a parlay bet? +
A parlay bet combines multiple individual bets into one wager. All selections must win for the parlay to pay out, but the potential returns are much higher than individual bets due to the increased difficulty. The odds multiply together to create larger potential payouts.
How are parlay odds calculated? +
Parlay odds are calculated by multiplying the odds of each individual bet together. For example, if you have three bets at +100 odds each, the parlay would pay approximately +700. Our AI helps identify correlated picks that work well together in parlays.
What's the difference between a parlay and a teaser? +
A teaser allows you to adjust point spreads in your favor across multiple games, but offers lower payouts than traditional parlays. Teasers are typically used for football and basketball, allowing you to move spreads by 6, 6.5, or 7 points.
How does AI generate parlay recommendations? +
Our AI analyzes correlations between different bets and identifies combinations with the best risk-adjusted expected value. We consider factors like game correlations, optimal bet sizing, and historical performance of similar parlay combinations.

UFC & MMA

How are UFC rankings determined? +
UFC rankings are determined by a panel of media members who vote based on fighter performance, quality of opposition, and recent activity. Rankings are updated after significant events and help determine title shot eligibility and fight booking priorities.
How does UFC scoring work? +
UFC uses the 10-point must system where the winner of each round gets 10 points and the loser gets 9 or fewer. Fights are scored on effective striking, effective grappling, control, and aggression. Three judges score each round independently.
What are the different UFC weight classes? +
UFC weight classes range from Strawweight (115 lbs) to Heavyweight (265 lbs), with separate divisions for men and women. Each division has specific weight limits that fighters must meet during official weigh-ins before their fights.
What UFC betting options are available? +
UFC betting includes moneyline (winner), over/under rounds, method of victory, round betting, and prop bets on specific fight outcomes. Our AI analyzes fighter styles, recent performance, and historical matchup data to identify value bets.

Player Prop Bets

What are player prop bets? +
Player prop bets are wagers on individual player statistics rather than game outcomes. Examples include over/under on points scored, rebounds, assists, passing yards, or touchdowns. These bets focus on individual performance within the game.
How does AI help with prop bets? +
Our AI analyzes player trends, matchup advantages, usage rates, and game script predictions to identify props with positive expected value. We consider factors like pace of play, defensive rankings, and player motivation levels.
What types of prop bets are available? +
Common prop bets include scoring props (points, touchdowns), statistical props (rebounds, assists, yards), and game-specific props (first to score, longest completion). Props vary by sport and are available for most major league games.
